Switching Roles

Role Chat is all about pretending to be someone who you are not. We wanted to allow for people to really express themselves through roleplaying- which isn't very fun nor interesting if there is only one option. In the above section, you are able to select different modifiers to form your Role Chat persona. Would you like to be Strong jimBoy89 The Wizard? Or perhaps Smart jimBoy89 The Goblin is a better fit for you. Whatever the case, there are plenty of roles you can choose from to take with you into the chat room!

Unlocking New Roles

Need more roles? No worries, there are plenty of roles that you can unlock through gaining fame in the chatroom. If somebody likes your deal, they are able to click on your username to boost your fame level. Reach different levels of fame to unlock super cool roles such as British you The Velociraptor or Conservative you The Liberal! The possibilities are endless! (Disclaimer: this previous statement was meant to be taken as more of a figure of speech as opposed to there literally being endless options. I mean come on, what do you think this is, Garry's Mod?)
