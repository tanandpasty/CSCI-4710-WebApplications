Switching Roles

Role Chat is all about pretending to be someone who you are not. We wanted to allow for people to really express themselves through roleplaying- which isn't very fun nor interesting if there is only one option. In the above section, you are able to select different modifiers to form your Role Chat persona. Would you like to be Tough jimBoy89 The Wizard? Or perhaps Smart jimBoy89 The Alien is a better fit for you. Whatever the case, there are plenty of roles you can choose from to take with you into the chat room!
